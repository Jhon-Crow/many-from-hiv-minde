## Cryptocurrency (Most Accessible Method)

### Legal Status in Russia (2025-2026)
- Cryptocurrencies are recognized as property/digital financial assets
- NOT legal tender - cannot be used for domestic payments
- Exception: digital currency permitted for international trade (law passed 2024)
- New framework being finalized by July 1, 2026
- Non-qualified investors limited to 300,000 RUB (~$3,300) per intermediary/year
- Transactions over 600,000 RUB must be reported to tax authorities

## E-Wallets

### YooMoney (formerly Yandex.Money)
- **Status**: Operational with limitations
- **International transfers**: Limited (USA transfers unavailable)
- **Best for**: Small transactions, domestic freelance payments
- **Warning**: Strict new verification requirements (May 2025 regulation changes)
- **URL**: https://yookassa.ru/

### QIWI
- **Status**: NOT operational since February 2024
- **Reason**: Central Bank of Russia revoked QIWI Bank's license
- **Details**: Found to violate anti-money laundering requirements

### WebMoney
- **Status**: Partially operational
- **Ruble wallets (WMR/WMP)**: NOT available (bank license revoked Feb 2022)
- **Available**: Dollar (WMZ), Euro (WME), Tenge, crypto wallets
- **Functions**: Transfers between wallets, P2P to bank cards, crypto exchange
